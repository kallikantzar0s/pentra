01 SERVICES

Attack Surface.

We cover every angle — from your perimeter to your people. Each engagement is custom-scoped, manually executed, and documented for both technical teams and executives.

We replicate the tactics of real threat actors targeting your organization from the outside. Every internet-facing asset is fair game — web servers, mail gateways, VPNs, DNS, cloud endpoints, APIs, and forgotten subdomains buried in your DNS history.

This isn't a vulnerability scan. Our operators manually chain low-severity findings into high-impact attack paths that automated tools will never flag.

//
OSINT & Reconnaissance

Full external footprint mapping — leaked credentials, exposed services, acquisition assets, and shadow IT discovery through passive and active recon.

//
Exploitation & Impact

We exploit what we find to prove real business impact — data exfiltration, service disruption, pivot to internal networks — not theoretical risk ratings.

//
Actionable Reporting

Every finding comes with proof-of-concept, CVSS scoring, reproduction steps, and remediation guidance your team can act on the same day.

We simulate what happens after an attacker gets inside — through phishing, a rogue employee, a compromised VPN, or physical access. Starting from a standard workstation or network jack, we map the blast radius.

The goal: demonstrate how far a motivated attacker can go from a single foothold, and what sensitive data they can reach along the way.

//
Privilege Escalation

From standard user to domain admin — we exploit misconfigurations, credential harvesting, Kerberos weaknesses, and local privilege escalation vectors.

//
Lateral Movement

Pass-the-hash, token impersonation, NTLM relay, Kerberoasting — we move through your network the same way APTs do.

//
Segmentation Validation

We test whether your network boundaries actually hold. Can we jump from corporate to production? From guest Wi-Fi to domain controllers?

Your web apps are your most exposed attack surface. We go far beyond OWASP Top 10 checklists — our testers manually probe every input, endpoint, workflow, and business logic path. We test like attackers think.

Covers REST APIs, GraphQL, WebSocket endpoints, SPAs, server-side rendering, microservices, and legacy monoliths alike.

//
Injection & Execution

SQLi, XSS, SSTI, command injection, SSRF, XXE, insecure deserialization — tested manually with custom payloads, not just scanner output.

//
Auth & Access Control

We break authentication flows, test MFA bypasses, exploit IDOR/BOLA vulnerabilities, and validate role-based access control at every endpoint.

//
Business Logic Flaws

The vulnerabilities scanners can't find — price manipulation, workflow bypass, race conditions, and state management issues that lead to real compromise.

Your firewall doesn't matter if someone clicks the link. We run intelligence-driven phishing campaigns that mirror the exact threats your employees face — from mass credential harvesting to targeted spear-phishing of executives.

We also test physical security, vishing (voice phishing), and pretexting scenarios when authorized.

//
Custom Campaigns

OSINT-driven pretexts mimicking your vendors, partners, and internal tools. We clone login pages, craft believable lures, and track every interaction.

//
Payload Testing

Macro-enabled docs, HTA, ISO/LNK, HTML smuggling — we test whether malicious payloads bypass your email gateway and endpoint protection.

//
Metrics & Reporting

Click rates, credential submissions, reporting rates, time-to-click — broken down by department, role, and campaign variant with trend analysis.

Active Directory is the skeleton key to your entire environment — and it's almost always misconfigured. We map every attack path from compromised user to Domain Admin using BloodHound, PingCastle, and custom tooling, then systematically close them.

This isn't a checklist audit. We attack your AD the same way ransomware operators do, then harden it against those exact techniques.

//
Attack Path Mapping

BloodHound analysis, ACL abuse chains, Kerberos delegation issues, GPO weaponization paths — every route to DA, visualized and prioritized.

//
Kerberos Hardening

Tiered Admin Model

We implement Microsoft's tier model, PAW deployment, LAPS, Credential Guard, LDAP signing, and channel binding — a prioritized hardening roadmap.

The ultimate test. Red Team Operations simulate a full adversary campaign — no scope limits, no announced timeline. We use the same TTPs as nation-state actors and ransomware gangs to test your people, processes, and technology under realistic conditions.

The goal isn't a list of vulnerabilities. It's answering: "Can our security program detect, respond to, and stop a real attack?"
